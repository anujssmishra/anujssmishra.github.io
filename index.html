About Me

I am a dedicated PhD Scholar at the Indian Institute of Management Lucknow, specializing in Information Technology & Systems. My research is situated at the intersection of technology, security, and business, focusing on the transformative potential of Distributed Ledger Technologies (DLT).

My primary research interests include the security and governance of blockchain systems, cybersecurity, cyber risk management, DLT in telecommunications, and the evolution of Web 3.0 and the Metaverse. I am passionate about developing robust frameworks that enhance the security and reliability of emerging digital ecosystems.
